The ✦ format

Our Research Format

01

Pick a real problem

Not a toy dataset. Something you or people around you actually hit. Real problems are the essential raw material — the skill successful founders, researchers, and builders share.

02

Dig to the core

Understand the problem at its root, not its symptoms. Read what exists, argue about it at the weekly gathering, and find the gap worth attacking.

03

Build the smallest fix

Implementation first. A rough working thing beats a polished plan — this is where "1 step > 1000 thoughts" stops being a quote and starts being a commit.

04

Write the paper

Turn what you built and learned into a portfolio-worthy research paper. We help with structure, citations, and honest results — no fluff.

05

Present + defend

Present it to the club, take the questions, defend the ideas. Then we feature it on the notokenclub site and socials for real exposure.
