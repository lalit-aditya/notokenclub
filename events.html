The story ✦ why this exists

We're just a bunch of tech-curious people in Chennai who got tired of having no one to seriously talk about AI and tech with. So we built our own space — a weekly gathering where curious minds dig into what's happening in AI, technology, and business, and build meaningful connections with people who are just as serious about growth.

No fluff. No gatekeeping. Just real conversations with the right people — over chai, without a sales pitch. Community-first, curiosity-driven, Chennai-rooted. And now Coimbatore-rooted too.
